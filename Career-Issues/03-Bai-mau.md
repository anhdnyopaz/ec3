> **Mục đích:** xem cách lắp **từ vựng (File 1)** + **lời khuyên & câu điều kiện (File 2)** thành bài hoàn chỉnh.
> **Quy ước in chữ:** **cấu trúc lời khuyên** in **đậm** (should/ought to/had better/why don't you/how about) · ***câu điều kiện If*** in đậm-nghiêng · _từ vựng chủ đề_ in _nghiêng_.
> **Cách dùng:** (1) Đọc hiểu → (2) Đóng vai / nói lại → (3) Đổi ví dụ thành chuyện của bạn.

# 🗣️ PHẦN A – HỘI THOẠI MẪU (Asking for advice at work)

## 💬 Hội thoại 1 — *"I feel so stressed!"* (Xin lời khuyên khi căng thẳng)

> **Lan:** Hi Minh, you look tired. Are you okay?
> **Minh:** Not really. I have _a busy schedule_ this week and I keep _working overtime_. I really _feel stressed_.
> **Lan:** I'm sorry to hear that. Can I give you some advice?
> **Minh:** Yes, please. What **should** I do?
> **Lan:** Well, first, you **should** _take a break_ every hour. ***If*** you rest a little, you **will** _feel_ better.
> **Minh:** That makes sense. But I still have so much to do.
> **Lan:** **Why don't you** _make a to-do list_ and _set priorities_? **How about** _doing_ the most important task first?
> **Minh:** Good idea. I always try to do everything at once.
> **Lan:** That's the problem. You**'d better not** _work overtime_ every day, or you will get _burnout_. ***If*** you _manage your time_ well, you **can** finish on time.
> **Minh:** Thank you, Lan. I **should** also _ask for advice_ more often!

> **🔑 Lời khuyên:** should, why don't you, how about, had better not. **Điều kiện:** If you rest…, If you manage your time… **Từ vựng:** busy schedule, work overtime, feel stressed, take a break, to-do list, set priorities, manage time.

---

## 💬 Hội thoại 2 — *"I keep making mistakes"* (Xin lời khuyên khi mắc lỗi)

> **Nam:** Can I ask you for some advice?
> **Boss:** Of course. What's wrong?
> **Nam:** I _make mistakes_ in my reports, and I think there is _poor communication_ in our team.
> **Boss:** I see. **It's a good idea to** _check your work_ before sending it. And ***if*** you don't understand a task, you **should** _ask for advice_ immediately.
> **Nam:** You're right. Sometimes I'm afraid to ask.
> **Boss:** Don't worry. **You ought to** _communicate more clearly_ with the team. ***If*** we all _communicate clearly_, we **will** _make fewer mistakes_.
> **Nam:** Thank you. I want to _improve my performance_.
> **Boss:** Great attitude. Know your _strengths and weaknesses_, and you **will** get better every month.

> **🔑 Lời khuyên:** It's a good idea to, should, ought to. **Điều kiện:** If you don't understand…, If we communicate clearly… **Từ vựng:** make mistakes, poor communication, ask for advice, communicate clearly, improve performance, strengths and weaknesses.

# ✍️ PHẦN B – ĐOẠN VĂN MẪU (Model paragraph, ≈130 từ, A2–B1)

## *A problem at work and how to solve it*

Many people today have _a busy schedule_ and often _work overtime_. As a result, they _feel stressed_ and sometimes _make mistakes_. In my opinion, there are simple ways to solve this problem.

First, you **should** _manage your time_ well. ***If*** you _make a to-do list_ and _set priorities_, you **will** finish important tasks first. Second, you **should** _take a break_ during the day, **because** a short rest helps your brain relax.

Moreover, when there is _poor communication_, you **ought to** _ask for advice_ and _communicate clearly_ with your team. ***If*** everyone works together, the team **will** _make fewer mistakes_.

In conclusion, ***if*** we _manage our time_, _take breaks_, and know our _strengths and weaknesses_, we **can** _improve our performance_ and enjoy our work more.

> **🔑 Lời khuyên:** should (×2), ought to. **Điều kiện:** If you make a to-do list…, If everyone works together…, If we manage our time… **Từ vựng:** busy schedule, work overtime, feel stressed, make mistakes, manage time, take a break, poor communication, ask for advice, strengths and weaknesses, improve performance (đủ 10 cụm lõi).

# 🎤 PHẦN C – THUYẾT TRÌNH ~2 PHÚT (≈255 từ)

## *How to deal with stress and problems at work*

> Hello everyone! My name is ______. Today, I'd like to talk about a common problem at work: _stress_ — and how we can deal with it.

> These days, many people have _a busy schedule_ and often _work overtime_. Because of this, they _feel stressed_, _make mistakes_, and slowly lose their energy. So, what **should** we do?

> First, we **should** _manage our time_ well. ***If*** you _make a to-do list_ and _set priorities_ every morning, you **will** know what is most important. **Why don't you** start your day with the hardest task? ***If*** you finish it early, you **will** feel more relaxed.

> Second, remember to _take a break_. **It's a good idea to** rest for a few minutes every hour. ***If*** you _take short breaks_, you **can** stay focused and _make fewer mistakes_.

> Third, don't be afraid to _ask for advice_. ***If*** there is _poor communication_ in your team, you **ought to** _communicate more clearly_ and ask your colleagues for help.

> ***Of course***, we cannot remove all stress. ***However***, ***if*** we know our _strengths and weaknesses_, we **can** keep improving.

> ***In conclusion***, ***if*** we _manage our time_, _take breaks_, and _ask for advice_, we **will** _improve our performance_ and feel happier at work. Thank you for listening!

> **🔑 Lời khuyên:** should (×2), why don't you, it's a good idea to, ought to. **Điều kiện:** If you make a to-do list…, If you take short breaks…, If we know our strengths…, If we manage our time… **Tương phản:** Of course, However.

## 🧱 KHUNG ĐIỀN của riêng bạn (Templates)

### 🗣️ Khung hội thoại (xin lời khuyên)
```
A: You look tired. Are you okay?
B: Not really. I ________ (vấn đề: work overtime / feel stressed…).
A: Can I give you some advice? You should ________ (giải pháp).
   Why don't you ________? / How about ________ (V-ing)?
B: Good idea. If I ________ , I will ________ .
A: Exactly. And you'd better not ________ , or ________ .
B: Thank you! I should also ________ .
```

### ✍️ Khung đoạn văn / thuyết trình
```
[Mở đầu]   Today I'd like to talk about a problem at work: ________ .
[Vấn đề]   Many people ________ (work overtime / busy schedule), so they ________ .
[Giải pháp 1] First, you should ________ . If you ________ , you will ________ .
[Giải pháp 2] Second, ________ . It's a good idea to ________ .
[Giải pháp 3] Third, why don't you ________ ? / you ought to ________ .
[Nhượng bộ]  Of course, ________ . However, ________ .
[Kết]       In conclusion, if we ________ , we will improve our performance. Thank you!
```

---

## 💡 Mẹo đạt B1

1. Mỗi giải pháp = **1 lời khuyên** (should/why don't you…) + **1 câu điều kiện** (If…, … will/should…).
2. Dùng **ít nhất 2 cách khuyên khác nhau** — đừng lặp lại *should* cả bài.
3. Luôn có mạch: **vấn đề → điều kiện → giải pháp → kết quả tốt** (*improve performance*).
4. Kết bài dùng 1 câu điều kiện tổng: *If we…, we will improve our performance.*
5. Viết/nói xong, **đọc to** để kiểm tra độ trôi chảy.

---

## ✅ Checklist trước khi nói/nộp

- [ ] Nêu rõ **1 vấn đề** ở đầu (feel stressed / work overtime / make mistakes…).
- [ ] Có **≥ 2 cách đưa lời khuyên** khác nhau (should / why don't you / had better / how about…).
- [ ] Có **≥ 2 câu điều kiện loại 1** (If + hiện tại đơn, … will/should…).
- [ ] Dùng **≥ 5 từ vựng** chủ đề (gồm cả các cụm trong 10 cụm lõi).
- [ ] Kết thúc bằng **giải pháp/kết quả tích cực** (*improve performance* / feel better).
- [ ] Nói được **≈ 2 phút** không nhìn giấy (nếu thuyết trình).
