# 🤖 Topic 03 – AI in the Workplace & Education | File 4: THUYẾT TRÌNH MẪU 2 PHÚT

> **Mục đích:** 4 kịch bản thuyết trình ~2 phút về AI — **2 bài ủng hộ (tích cực)** + **2 bài lo ngại (tiêu cực)**, phủ cả **công việc** và **giáo dục** — để luyện **thuyết trình** và **phản biện**.
> **Quy ước in chữ:** **modal** in **đậm** · ***tương phản*** in đậm-nghiêng · _từ vựng chủ đề_ in nghiêng.
> **Cách dùng:** học **ý chính** (không học vẹt), đổi ví dụ cho hợp với bạn, nói lại không nhìn giấy.

## ⏱️ Phân bổ thời gian 2 phút (≈ 250–280 từ)

# ✅ PHẦN A – 2 BÀI TÍCH CỰC

## ✍️ Bài 1 — *AI helps us work better* (Workplace, ≈255 từ)

> Hello everyone! My name is ______. Today, I'd like to talk about how _artificial intelligence_ helps us at work. **In my opinion**, AI is very useful in the _workplace_.

> First, AI **can** _save time and increase productivity_, **because** it works very fast and never gets tired. ***While*** a person needs to rest, a machine **can** work all day and all night.

> Second, AI **can** _reduce human error_. **Since** machines do not forget or feel sleepy, they make fewer mistakes than people. This is very important in jobs like banking and medicine.

> Third, AI **can** _boost customer service_. Many companies now use chatbots that **can** answer questions 24/7, **so** customers do not have to wait.

> ***Of course***, AI is not perfect. ***However***, ***despite*** some problems, I believe AI **will** make our work easier and faster.

> To use it well, we **should** see AI as a _helpful tool_, not as a replacement for people.

> ***In conclusion***, AI is great for the workplace **because** it saves time, reduces errors, and improves customer service. **As long as** we use it _wisely_, AI **can** help everyone work better. Thank you for listening!

> **🔑 Modal:** can (×5), will, should. **Tương phản:** While, However, Despite. **Lý do:** because (×3), since, so.

---

## ✍️ Bài 2 — *AI is a great learning assistant* (Education, ≈255 từ)

> Hello everyone! I'm ______. Today I want to share why I think AI is good for students. **I believe** AI **can** be a great _learning assistant_.

> First, AI **can** _support students anytime_. **Because** an AI tutor is always available, students **can** ask questions even at night. They **can** also _plan a learning schedule_ that fits them.

> Second, AI helps us improve our English. It **can** help us _practice speaking & listening_ and _improve our writing_, **since** it gives _instant feedback_. ***While*** a teacher has many students, AI **can** give _personalized learning_ to each one.

> Third, AI **can** make study more fun. **Because** it uses games and videos, students **can** _stay motivated with fun learning_ and _track their progress_ easily.

> ***Of course***, ***however***, AI **may** cause problems too. Some students **might** use it to cheat. ***Despite*** this, I still think the benefits are stronger.

> So, we **should** use AI to learn, not to copy. We **shouldn't** let it think for us.

> ***In conclusion***, AI is a wonderful tool in _education_ **because** it supports students, improves skills, and makes learning fun. **As long as** we use it _responsibly_, AI **can** help us study better. Thank you!

> **🔑 Modal:** can (×7), may, might, should, shouldn't. **Tương phản:** While, However, Despite. **Lý do:** because (×3), since.

# ❌ PHẦN B – 2 BÀI LO NGẠI

## ✍️ Bài 3 — *AI may take away our jobs* (Workplace, ≈255 từ)

> Hello everyone! My name is ______. Today, I'd like to talk about a worry about AI in the _workplace_. **In my opinion**, AI **can** also bring serious problems.

> First, AI **may** _replace workers_. **Because** machines **can** do many tasks faster and cheaper, some companies **might** not need people anymore. ***As a result***, many workers **may** _lose their jobs_.

> Second, people **may** _over-rely on AI_. ***While*** AI is helpful, workers **might** stop learning and slowly _lose their skills_, **because** the machine does everything for them.

> Third, AI **can** make mistakes. **Since** it only learns from _data_, it **may** be _wrong_ or _biased_, and this **can** be dangerous in important jobs.

> ***Of course***, AI also has benefits. ***However***, ***despite*** these benefits, we must be careful about the risks.

> To stay safe, workers **should** learn new _skills_ and **should** not depend on AI for everything. Companies **should** use AI to _help_ people, not to remove them.

> ***In conclusion***, AI **may** be harmful in the workplace **because** it can cause _job loss_, make us lose skills, and sometimes be wrong. ***Although*** AI is powerful, we **should** always keep humans at the centre. Thank you for listening!

> **🔑 Modal:** can, may, might, should. **Tương phản:** While, However, Despite, Although. **Lý do:** because (×3), since.

---

## ✍️ Bài 4 — *Too much AI is bad for learning* (Education, ≈250 từ)

> Hello everyone! I'm ______. Today I want to talk about why too much AI **can** be bad for students. **I believe** AI in _education_ has some real dangers.

> First, AI **can** make cheating easy. **Because** AI **can** write essays and answer questions in seconds, some students **may** copy its work. ***As a result***, they do not really learn.

> Second, students **might** _over-rely on AI_. ***While*** it is good to get help, learners **may** stop thinking for themselves, **because** the AI gives the answer immediately. Slowly, they **could** _lose important skills_, like writing and problem-solving.

> Third, AI **can** be wrong. **Since** it learns only from _data_, it **may** give false information, and young students **might** believe it without checking.

> ***Of course***, AI also helps learning. ***However***, ***despite*** its benefits, we must use it carefully.

> So, students **should** use AI only to check and practise, not to do the work for them. Teachers **should** teach them to think first and use AI later.

> ***In conclusion***, too much AI **can** harm education **because** it makes cheating easy, reduces thinking skills, and can spread wrong information. ***Although*** AI is useful, we **should** use it _responsibly_. Thank you for listening!

> **🔑 Modal:** can, may, might, could, should. **Tương phản:** While, However, Despite, Although. **Lý do:** because (×3), since.

## 🧱 Khung điền chỗ trống (kịch bản 2 phút của RIÊNG bạn)

```
Hello everyone! My name is __________.
Today, I'd like to talk about AI in __________ (the workplace / education).
In my opinion, AI is __________ (good / bad) because __________.

First, AI can __________ because __________.

Second, AI can / may __________. Since __________, __________.

Third, although __________ (mặt còn lại), AI can / might __________.

Of course, however, __________ (nhượng bộ).
We should __________ (lời khuyên). We shouldn't __________.

In conclusion, AI is __________ because __________, __________, and __________.
As long as we use it wisely, __________.
Thank you for listening!
```

## 🎤 Mẹo NÓI tự nhiên & rõ ràng

1. **Đừng đọc giấy** — học thuộc *ý chính* của 3 luận điểm.
2. **Ngắt cụm:** *Although AI can help, / we should / still think for ourselves.*
3. **Nhấn modal + từ vựng chính:** *AI CAN reDUCE HUman Error.*
4. **Nói chậm, rõ** — 2 phút chỉ cần ~250–280 từ.
5. **Dùng đủ loại modal:** can (lợi ích) · may/might (rủi ro) · should (lời khuyên).
6. **Tín hiệu chuyển ý:** First… Second… Third… However… In conclusion…

---

## ✅ Checklist trước khi thuyết trình

- [ ] Nêu rõ **quan điểm** ở mở đầu.
- [ ] Có **3 luận điểm**, mỗi luận điểm có **modal + lý do**.
- [ ] Dùng **≥ 3 loại modal** khác nhau (can / may-might / should).
- [ ] Có **≥ 1 câu tương phản** (although/however/despite) để nhượng bộ.
- [ ] Dùng **≥ 5 từ vựng** chủ đề (gồm cả 12 cụm lõi).
- [ ] Kết bài **nhắc lại quan điểm** + lời khuyên (**should** / *use it wisely*).
- [ ] Nói được **≈ 2 phút** không nhìn giấy.
