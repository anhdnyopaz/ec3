# 🤖 Topic 03 – AI in the Workplace & Education | File 3: BÀI LUẬN MẪU (Model Essays)

> **Mục đích:** tham khảo cách viết thành đoạn/bài hoàn chỉnh về AI.
> **Quy ước in chữ:** **modal verbs** in **đậm** (can/could/will/may/should) · từ chỉ **lý do** in **đậm** · ***tương phản*** in đậm-nghiêng · _từ vựng chủ đề_ in nghiêng.
> **Cách dùng:** (1) Đọc hiểu → (2) Dịch ngược ra tiếng Anh → (3) Viết bản của riêng bạn.

## ✍️ Essay 1 — *The Benefits of AI in the Workplace* (≈130 từ, A2–B1)

Today, more and more companies use _artificial intelligence_ at work. In my opinion, AI has many _benefits_.

First, AI **can** _save time and increase productivity_ **because** it works very fast and never gets tired. Second, it **can** _reduce human error_, **since** machines do not forget or feel sleepy. Many shops also use chatbots to _boost customer service_, **because** a chatbot **can** answer questions 24/7.

Moreover, AI **can** _predict problems_ and _improve decision-making_, so managers **can** choose better plans.

***Of course***, AI is not perfect. ***However***, **if** we use it wisely, it **will** make our work easier and faster.

In conclusion, AI is a _powerful tool_ in the workplace **because** it saves time, reduces mistakes, and helps people make better decisions.

> **🔑 Modal:** can (×4), will. **Lý do:** because (×3), since. **Tương phản:** However.

## ✍️ Essay 2 — *AI in Education: Help or Harm?* (≈150 từ, B1)

AI is now used in many schools, but people often argue whether it is good or bad for students.

***On the one hand***, AI has many _benefits_. It **can** _support students anytime_, **because** an AI tutor is always available. It also helps learners _practice speaking & listening_ and _improve their writing_, **since** it **can** give _instant feedback_. With AI, students **can** _plan a learning schedule_ and _stay motivated with fun learning_.

***On the other hand***, AI **may** cause problems. Some students **might** use it to cheat, and others **may** _over-rely on AI_ and stop thinking for themselves. AI **can** also be wrong, **because** it only learns from _data_.

In my opinion, AI is helpful **only if** we use it _responsibly_. Schools **should** teach students how to use AI as a tool, not as a replacement for their own brain.

> **🔑 Modal:** can (×5), may, might, should. **Tương phản:** On the one hand / On the other hand. **Lý do:** because (×3), since.

---

## ✍️ Essay 3 — *"AI will replace human workers." Do you agree?* (≈150 từ, B1 – nêu ý kiến)

Some people believe that AI **will** replace human workers in the future. ***However***, I do not completely agree with this idea.

It is true that AI **can** do many tasks. It **can** _automate_ boring work, _reduce human error_, and _save costs_. ***Because*** of this, some jobs **may** disappear, and a few workers **might** _lose their jobs_.

***Nevertheless***, I think AI **cannot** replace people completely. Machines **can't** feel emotions or be truly creative, **so** we still need humans for difficult and caring jobs, such as teachers, nurses, and artists.

In my opinion, AI **will** change jobs rather than destroy them. Workers **should** learn new _skills_ and **should** see AI as a helpful _tool_.

***All in all***, I believe AI and humans **can** work together, **as long as** we _keep up with technology_.

> **🔑 Modal:** will, can, may, might, can't, should. **Tương phản:** However / Nevertheless / All in all. **Lý do:** because, so.

## 🧱 Khung viết essay cho riêng bạn (Template)

> Điền vào chỗ trống để có bài của chính bạn.

**Mở bài:** Today, AI is used in ______ (the workplace / education). It has both good and bad sides.

**Đoạn lợi ích:** First, AI **can** ______ **because** ______. It **can** also ______, **since** ______.

**Đoạn lo ngại:** ***However***, AI **may** ______. Some people **might** ______ **because of** ______.

**Đoạn lời khuyên:** In my opinion, we **should** ______. We **shouldn't** ______.

**Kết bài:** In conclusion, AI is ______ **as long as** we use it ______.

## 💡 Mẹo viết đạt B1

1. Mỗi đoạn = **1 ý chính + 1–2 lý do** (because/since) + **ít nhất 1 modal** (can/may/should).
2. Dùng **modal khác nhau**: *can* (lợi ích) · *may/might* (rủi ro) · *should* (lời khuyên) · *will* (tương lai).
3. Có **1 cặp tương phản** (On the one hand / However…) để cân hai mặt.
4. Kết bài **nêu rõ quan điểm** + 1 lời khuyên (*should… / as long as…*).
5. Viết xong, **đọc to** để kiểm tra độ trôi chảy.
