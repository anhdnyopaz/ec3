# 🎬 Topic 02 – Entertainment | File 3: THUYẾT TRÌNH MẪU 2 PHÚT – *6 bài (3 tích cực + 3 tiêu cực)*

> **Mục đích:** cho bạn **6 kịch bản thuyết trình hoàn chỉnh ~2 phút** về giải trí với giới trẻ — **3 bài bênh vực (tích cực)** và **3 bài phản đối (tiêu cực)** — để luyện **thuyết trình** và **phản biện**.
>
> **Quy ước in chữ:** từ **tương phản/nhượng bộ** in ***đậm-nghiêng*** (although, however, despite…) · từ chỉ **lý do** in **đậm** (because, since, due to) · **từ vựng chủ đề** in _nghiêng_.
> **Cách dùng:** (1) Đọc to cảm nhận nhịp → (2) Học **ý chính**, không học vẹt → (3) Đổi ví dụ cho đúng với bạn → (4) Nói lại không nhìn giấy.

---

## ⏱️ Phân bổ thời gian 2 phút (≈ 250–280 từ nói)

| Phần | Thời gian | Nội dung |
|---|---|---|
| 👋 Mở đầu | ~15 giây | Chào + nêu **quan điểm** (tích cực hay tiêu cực) |
| 1️⃣ Luận điểm 1 | ~30 giây | 1 ý + **lý do** (because/since) |
| 2️⃣ Luận điểm 2 | ~30 giây | 1 ý + **lý do** |
| 3️⃣ Luận điểm 3 | ~25 giây | 1 ý + **lý do** |
| ⚖️ Nhượng bộ | ~20 giây | Thừa nhận mặt còn lại (***Although / However***) |
| 🙏 Kết | ~15 giây | Nhắc lại **quan điểm** + cảm ơn |

> 🎯 **Bí quyết ăn điểm:** mỗi luận điểm phải có **lý do** (because…), và phải có **1 câu nhượng bộ** (Although…) để thể hiện bạn nhìn được cả hai mặt.

# ✅ PHẦN A – 3 BÀI TÍCH CỰC (Entertainment is GOOD for young people)

## ✍️ Bài 1 — *Entertainment helps young people relax and relieve stress* (≈260 từ)

> Hello everyone! My name is ______. Today, I'd like to talk about why entertainment is good for young people. **In my opinion**, entertainment is very important **because** it helps us _relax_.

> First, entertainment helps young people _relieve stress_. After a long day at school, students feel tired and worried. ***Although*** they still have a lot of homework, they need some time to rest. Watching a funny video or _listening to music_ is a _healthy way to relax_, **because** it makes us feel happy again.

> Second, entertainment is _enjoyable_ and _affordable_. Many forms of entertainment, such as _music_ and online _videos_, are free or cheap. ***Even though*** we do not have much money, we can still _enjoy_ them _in our free time_.

> Third, entertainment gives us energy to study better. ***While*** some people think it is a _waste of time_, I believe a short break is good for our brain. **Since** our mind feels fresh after we _relax_, we can focus more on our lessons.

> Of course, we should use entertainment _in moderation_. ***Although*** it is fun, too much _screen time_ is not good. We should _set a time limit_ and _balance fun and study_.

> ***In conclusion***, entertainment is good for young people **because** it helps us _relax_, it is _affordable_, and it gives us energy. **As long as** we use it wisely, entertainment makes our life happier. Thank you for listening!

> **🔑 Tương phản:** Although, Even though, While. **Lý do:** because (×3), since.

---

## ✍️ Bài 2 — *Entertainment boosts creativity and skills* (≈255 từ)

> Hello everyone! I'm ______, and today I want to share why I think entertainment is good for young people. **In my view**, entertainment does not only bring fun — it also helps us learn.

> First, many forms of entertainment _boost creativity_. ***Although*** people often say _video games_ are useless, I disagree. **Because** games and creative apps ask us to think and solve problems, they help young people become more _creative_.

> Second, entertainment can _improve skills_. ***For example***, ***while*** we watch English films or listen to songs, we learn new words without feeling bored. **Since** the content is _enjoyable_, we want to learn more. Some teenagers even learn to draw, dance, or make videos from online _content_.

> Third, entertainment helps us _make friends_ and _broaden the mind_. **Because** young people share the same hobbies, they connect easily. ***However***, friendship is not the only benefit; entertainment also shows us new cultures and ideas around the world.

> I know some people worry about _addiction_. ***Despite*** this problem, I still believe the good sides are stronger, **as long as** we choose good content and use it _in moderation_.

> ***To sum up***, entertainment is good for young people **because** it _boosts creativity_, _improves skills_, and helps us _make friends_. ***Although*** it has some risks, it can make us smarter and more open-minded. Thank you very much!

> **🔑 Tương phản:** Although, While, However, Despite. **Lý do:** because (×3), since.

## ✍️ Bài 3 — *Entertainment connects friends and brings people together* (≈250 từ)

> Hello everyone! My name is ______. Today, I'd like to talk about how entertainment connects young people. **I believe** entertainment is good **because** it brings people closer.

> First, entertainment helps us _make friends_. ***While*** some teenagers are shy in real life, they can talk easily about a favourite _movie_, _song_, or _game_. **Since** they share the same _hobby_, they feel comfortable and become good friends.

> Second, entertainment brings families together. ***Although*** parents and children are often busy, watching a film or playing a _sport_ together at the weekend is a _relaxing_ way to spend time. **Because** they laugh and enjoy the moment, the family feels closer.

> Third, entertainment builds a sense of community. ***For example***, a _concert_ or a football match brings thousands of people together. **Due to** this shared joy, strangers can smile and cheer like friends.

> ***Of course***, ***however***, we should not let entertainment replace real life. ***Despite*** its benefits, spending too much time online can make us forget the people next to us. So we should _balance fun and study_ and meet friends face to face too.

> ***In conclusion***, entertainment is good for young people **because** it helps us _make friends_, brings families together, and builds community. ***As long as*** we keep a good balance, entertainment makes us happier and less lonely. Thank you for listening!

> **🔑 Tương phản:** While, Although, However, Despite. **Lý do:** because (×3), since, due to.

# ❌ PHẦN B – 3 BÀI TIÊU CỰC (Entertainment can be BAD for young people)

## ✍️ Bài 4 — *Entertainment can be addictive and waste time* (≈260 từ)

> Hello everyone! My name is ______. Today, I'd like to talk about a problem of entertainment with young people. **In my opinion**, entertainment can be harmful **because** it is often _addictive_.

> First, many forms of entertainment are very _addictive_. ***Although*** _video games_ and short videos are fun, they are designed to keep us watching. **Because** of this, many teenagers _spend hours online_ and cannot stop.

> Second, entertainment makes young people _waste time_. ***While*** students should study or sleep, they keep _scrolling_ on their phones. **Due to** this habit, they have less time for important things, such as homework and family.

> Third, too much entertainment is a big _distraction_. ***Even though*** a student wants to focus, a new notification or a funny clip pulls the attention away. **Since** the brain jumps from one thing to another, it is hard to study well.

> ***Of course***, entertainment is not all bad. ***However***, ***despite*** its good sides, the problem of _addiction_ is real and serious for many young people today.

> So, what should we do? I think we should _set a time limit_, turn off notifications, and _balance fun and study_.

> ***In conclusion***, entertainment can be harmful **because** it is _addictive_, it makes us _waste time_, and it is a _distraction_. ***Although*** we all enjoy it, we must use it _in moderation_ before it controls our life. Thank you for listening!

> **🔑 Tương phản:** Although, While, Even though, However, Despite. **Lý do:** because, because of, due to, since.

## ✍️ Bài 5 — *Entertainment can harm health and studies* (≈255 từ)

> Hello everyone! I'm ______. Today I want to talk about how entertainment can harm young people. **I believe** too much entertainment is bad **because** it affects our health and our studies.

> First, entertainment is bad for health. ***Although*** playing games at night feels relaxing, it makes teenagers _stay up late_. **Due to** this, they sleep badly and feel tired the next day. ***While*** _sport_ keeps us healthy, sitting and watching screens for hours makes us _lazy_ and _passive_.

> Second, entertainment can _affect studies_. **Because** young people spend too much time on fun, they have less time to learn. ***Even though*** they know exams are near, some students still choose games over books, and their grades drop.

> Third, too much _screen time_ hurts the eyes and the mind. **Since** we look at bright screens all day, our eyes get tired and we feel stressed instead of relaxed.

> ***Of course***, entertainment is not always harmful. ***However***, ***despite*** its benefits, we must be careful about how much time we spend on it.

> To protect ourselves, we should _set a time limit_, do some _sport_, and sleep early.

> ***In conclusion***, entertainment can harm young people **because** it damages our health, our studies, and our eyes. ***Although*** a little fun is fine, too much is dangerous. We should always _balance fun and study_. Thank you for listening!

> **🔑 Tương phản:** Although, While, Even though, However, Despite. **Lý do:** because (×2), due to, since.

## ✍️ Bài 6 — *Some entertainment content is harmful and makes teens passive* (≈255 từ)

> Hello everyone! My name is ______. Today, I'd like to talk about why some entertainment is bad for young people. **In my view**, the problem is not fun itself, but the _harmful content_ and bad habits it can bring.

> First, not all _content_ is safe. ***Although*** the internet has many good things, it also has _violent_ and _unhealthy content_. **Because** young people are still growing, this content can affect their thinking and behaviour.

> Second, some entertainment spreads bad trends. ***While*** a few _trends_ are creative, others are dangerous or silly. **Since** teenagers want to be popular, they may copy these _trends_ without thinking.

> Third, too much easy entertainment makes young people _passive_. ***Even though*** they have free time, many teenagers only _scroll_ and _watch_ instead of reading or creating. **Due to** this habit, they become _lazy_ and stop using their imagination.

> ***Of course***, I am not saying all entertainment is bad. ***However***, ***despite*** the fun, parents and schools should help teenagers choose good content.

> What can we do? We should choose _educational_ and positive content, follow safe _trends_, and spend time on real hobbies too.

> ***In conclusion***, some entertainment is bad for young people **because** of _harmful content_, dangerous _trends_, and _passive_ habits. ***Although*** entertainment can be good, we must choose wisely and use it _in moderation_. Thank you for listening!

> **🔑 Tương phản:** Although, While, Even though, However, Despite. **Lý do:** because, because of, since, due to.

## 🧱 Khung điền chỗ trống (kịch bản 2 phút của RIÊNG bạn)

> Chọn lập trường (tích cực **hoặc** tiêu cực), điền vào chỗ trống, rồi học thuộc **ý chính**.

```
Hello everyone! My name is __________.
Today, I'd like to talk about __________ (entertainment and young people).
In my opinion, entertainment is __________ (good / bad) because __________.

First, __________ (luận điểm 1) because __________ (lý do).

Second, __________ (luận điểm 2). Since __________ (lý do), __________.

Third, although __________ (mặt còn lại), __________ (luận điểm 3),
because of __________ (lý do).

Of course, however, __________ (nhượng bộ – thừa nhận mặt kia).
Despite __________, I still think __________.
We should __________ (lời khuyên: set a time limit / balance fun and study).

In conclusion, entertainment is __________ because __________,
__________, and __________. As long as we use it in moderation, __________.
Thank you for listening!
```

---

## 🎤 Mẹo NÓI tự nhiên & rõ ràng (2 phút)

1. **Đừng đọc từ giấy.** Học thuộc *ý chính* của 3 luận điểm, rồi nói bằng lời của mình.
2. **Ngắt cụm (chunking):** nghỉ nhẹ ở dấu phẩy. VD: *Although they have homework, / they need some time / to relax.*
3. **Nhấn từ quan trọng** (từ vựng chủ đề + từ tương phản): *aLTHOUGH it is FUN, it can be adDICtive.*
4. **Nói chậm hơn bạn nghĩ.** 2 phút chỉ cần ~250–280 từ; chậm + rõ > nhanh + vấp.
5. **Dùng câu đơn giản** (A2): S + V + O. Mỗi luận điểm 1 câu chính + 1 lý do.
6. **Tín hiệu chuyển ý** cho người nghe dễ theo: *First… Second… Third… However… In conclusion…*
7. **Nhìn người nghe & mỉm cười** → tự nhiên và tự tin hơn.

---

## ✅ Checklist trước khi thuyết trình

- [ ] Nêu rõ **quan điểm** ở mở đầu (good / bad).
- [ ] Có **3 luận điểm**, mỗi luận điểm kèm **≥ 1 lý do** (because / since / due to).
- [ ] Có **≥ 2 câu tương phản** (***although / however / despite / while***).
- [ ] Có **1 câu nhượng bộ** thừa nhận mặt còn lại (cho thấy nhìn 2 mặt).
- [ ] Dùng **≥ 5 từ vựng** chủ đề (cả ✅ và ❌ tùy bài).
- [ ] Kết bài **nhắc lại quan điểm** + lời khuyên (*in moderation / balance*).
- [ ] Nói được **≈ 2 phút** mà **không nhìn giấy**.
