## 📝 Phần A: Điền từ vựng về lợi ích (chọn trong hộp)

`benefit · stay connected · opportunities · community · awareness · educational · keep in touch · networking`

## 📝 Phần C: Nối thành 1 câu dùng từ trong ngoặc

1. Social media is useful. We can learn new skills. **(because)**
   → ________

   → ________

## 📝 Phần D: Sửa lỗi (Find the mistake)

1. She is happy because of she has many friends online. → ________
2. Teens are tired because late nights. → ________

## 📝 Phần E: Viết đoạn ngắn (mở)
Viết 4–5 câu trả lời: **"How does social media affect young people?"**
Yêu cầu: nêu **1 lợi ích + 1 tác hại**, dùng **ít nhất 2 từ chỉ lý do** (because / since / because of / due to).

---
---

## ✅ ĐÁP ÁN (Answer key)

**Phần A:** 1. keep in touch · 2. benefit · 3. educational · 4. networking · 5. stay connected · 6. opportunities · 7. awareness · 8. community

**Phần B:** 1. because · 2. because of · 3. because of · 4. because · 5. because of

**Phần C:** *(có thể diễn đạt khác chút)*
1. Social media is useful **because we can learn new skills.**
2. **Since it is free,** anyone can use it.
3. Many teens are anxious **because they compare themselves to others.**
4. He sleeps badly **due to using his phone at night** (hoặc *due to his phone use at night*).

**Phần D:**
1. ❌ *because of she has* → ✅ **because she has** (sau *because of* phải là danh từ, không có S+V).
2. ❌ *because late nights* → ✅ **because of late nights** / **because they have late nights**.

**Phần E:** câu mẫu tham khảo:
> *Social media affects young people in both good and bad ways. It is useful because they can stay connected and learn new skills. However, many teens feel stressed because of the pressure to look perfect, and some sleep badly due to late-night scrolling. So it is helpful if they use it in moderation.*
