# 📱 Topic 01 – Social Media | File 2: MẪU CÂU GIAO TIẾP

> **Trọng tâm:** nói về **lợi ích của MXH** & **ảnh hưởng đến giới trẻ**, có dùng **clauses of reason (vì/bởi vì)**.
> Học thuộc và thay từ in đậm bằng từ của bạn.

---

## 1️⃣ Nói về LỢI ÍCH của MXH (Benefits)

- Social media is useful **because** we can stay in touch with friends.
- One big benefit is **that we can learn new skills for free.**
- It's a great way to **share ideas and meet new people.**
- **Since** it's free, anyone can use it.
- It helps young people **express themselves and be creative.**

---

## 2️⃣ Nói MXH ảnh hưởng giới trẻ thế nào (Effects on young people)

**👍 Ảnh hưởng tích cực:**
- Social media helps teenagers **stay connected with their friends.**
- Young people can **learn languages and find job opportunities.**
- It gives them **a place to express themselves.**

**👎 Ảnh hưởng tiêu cực:**
- Many teenagers feel anxious **because they compare themselves to others.**
- Students waste study time **because of social media.**
- Some young people sleep badly **due to late-night scrolling.**
- It can lower their self-esteem **since everyone posts a "perfect" life.**

---

## 3️⃣ Giải thích LÝ DO (Clauses of reason – trọng tâm ngữ pháp)

**Khung câu dùng ngay:**
- ..., **because** + (chủ ngữ + động từ).
- **Since / As** + (chủ ngữ + động từ), ...
- ..., **because of / due to** + (danh từ).

**Ví dụ ghép:**
- I love Instagram **because it is fun and creative.**
- **Since** social media is so popular, almost every teenager uses it.
- Many teens are tired **because of late-night scrolling.**
- Their grades drop **due to social media addiction.**

---

## 4️⃣ Nêu ý kiến (Giving opinions) – cho B1

- I think social media is **good for young people if they use it carefully.**
- In my opinion, the benefits are **bigger than** the problems.
- For me, it's **useful but it can be harmful too.**

**Đồng ý / không đồng ý:**
- I agree, because... — Tôi đồng ý, vì...
- That's true, but... — Đúng vậy, nhưng...
- I see your point, but I think... — Tôi hiểu ý bạn, nhưng tôi nghĩ...

---
