# 📱 Topic 01 – Social Media | File 7: BÀI LUẬN MẪU (Model Essays)

> **Mục đích:** tham khảo cách viết/nói thành đoạn hoàn chỉnh.
> Các từ chỉ lý do được **in đậm**, từ vựng về *benefits* được _in nghiêng_.
> Cách dùng: (1) Đọc hiểu → (2) Dịch ngược ra tiếng Anh → (3) Viết bản của riêng bạn.

---

## ✍️ Essay 1 — The Benefits of Social Media (≈110 từ, A2–B1)

Social media has become a part of our daily life. It has many _benefits_. First, it helps us _stay connected_ with friends and family **because** we can chat with them anytime. Second, it is a powerful tool for learning. We can _learn new skills_ for free, **since** there is so much information online. Third, many people use social media to _promote a business_ **because** they can _reach a wide audience_ easily. In short, social media is very _useful_ and _convenient_ **due to** its many features. If we use it wisely, it can make our lives better.

**🔑 Cấu trúc lý do dùng trong bài:** because (×2), since, due to.
**📖 Từ nối:** First, Second, Third, In short.

## ✍️ Essay 2 — How Social Media Affects Young People (≈130 từ, B1)

Social media affects young people in both good and bad ways.

On the positive side, it helps teenagers _stay connected_ with their friends **because** they can talk online every day. They can also _learn new skills_ and _find job opportunities_ **since** the internet is full of useful _information_.

However, there are some problems too. Many young people feel stressed **because of** the pressure to look perfect online. Some of them sleep badly **due to** late-night scrolling, and their grades drop **because** they spend too much time on their phones.

In conclusion, social media is helpful for young people, but only if they use it _in moderation_. Parents and schools should also teach them how to use it in a healthy way.

**🔑 Cấu trúc lý do:** because (×2), since, because of, due to — đủ cả 5 dạng.
**📖 Bố cục:** Mở bài → Mặt tốt → Mặt xấu → Kết luận.

## ✍️ Essay 3 — Is Social Media Good or Bad? (≈120 từ, B1 – dạng nêu ý kiến)

In my opinion, social media is more good than bad **if** we use it carefully.

It has many _advantages_. For example, it _connects people around the world_ and gives us free _knowledge_. Students can study online **because** there are so many _educational_ videos. Small shops can also grow **due to** online advertising.

Of course, social media has bad sides. It can be addictive, and some people believe fake news **because** they do not check the facts. **Since** it takes a lot of time, it can also affect our study or work.

All in all, I think the _benefits_ are bigger than the problems, so social media is a good thing when we control how we use it.

**🔑 Cấu trúc lý do:** because (×2), since, due to.
**📖 Mẫu nêu ý kiến:** In my opinion, ... / All in all, I think ...

## 🧱 Khung viết essay cho riêng bạn (Template)

> Điền vào chỗ trống để có bài của chính mình.

**Mở bài:** Social media is ______ in our life. It has both good and bad sides.

**Đoạn lợi ích:** First, it helps us ______ **because** ______. Also, we can ______ **since** ______.

**Đoạn tác hại:** However, some people ______ **because of** ______. Others ______ **due to** ______.

**Kết bài:** In conclusion, I think social media is ______ if we use it ______.

## 💡 Mẹo viết đạt B1
1. Mỗi đoạn nên có **1 ý chính + 1–2 lý do** (dùng because/since/because of/due to).
2. Dùng **từ nối**: First, Second, Also, However, In conclusion.
3. Trộn câu ngắn và câu dài để bài tự nhiên hơn.
4. Viết xong, **đọc to** để kiểm tra có trôi chảy không.
