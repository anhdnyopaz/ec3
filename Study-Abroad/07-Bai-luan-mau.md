# 🎓 Topic – Study Abroad | File 7: BÀI LUẬN MẪU

> **Mục đích:** 3 bài luận mẫu (A2–B1) về du học + **khung viết** + **mẹo đạt B1**.
> **Quy ước:** **Present Perfect** in **đậm** · _từ vựng chủ đề_ in _nghiêng_ · ***từ nối/tương phản*** in đậm-nghiêng.
> Mỗi bài ≈ 150–180 từ, có mở – thân – kết rõ ràng.

---

## ✍️ BÀI 1 – Dạng NÊU Ý KIẾN (≈160 từ)
### *Do you think studying abroad is a good idea?*

In my opinion, _studying abroad_ is a great idea for young people today.

***Firstly***, it helps students _improve their language skills_. When you live in another country, you use English every day, so you learn faster. ***Secondly***, _studying abroad_ lets you _experience a new culture_ and _broaden your horizons_. You try new food, meet new people, and _open your mind_.

***Moreover***, living far from home teaches you to be _independent_. I **have** always _lived_ with my parents, but I believe _studying abroad_ would help me _grow up_ and become _self-reliant_.

***Of course***, there are some challenges. Students may _feel homesick_ or have a _language barrier_ at first. The _tuition fees_ are also _expensive_. ***However***, I think the benefits are greater than the difficulties.

***In conclusion***, _studying abroad_ is a wonderful, _once-in-a-lifetime experience_. If I get the chance, I will take it.

> **🔑 Từ nối:** Firstly, Secondly, Moreover, Of course, However, In conclusion. **Present Perfect:** have always lived. **Từ vựng:** study abroad, improve language skills, experience a new culture, broaden horizons, independent, feel homesick, language barrier, tuition fees.

---

## ✍️ BÀI 2 – Dạng ƯU & NHƯỢC ĐIỂM (≈170 từ)
### *The advantages and disadvantages of studying abroad*

_Studying abroad_ has become popular, but it has both _advantages and disadvantages_.

***On the one hand***, there are many benefits. Students get a _high-quality education_ and a valuable _degree_. They also _make international friends_ and gain _better career opportunities_ after they _graduate_. ***In addition***, they learn to be _independent_ because they live far from their families.

***On the other hand***, _studying abroad_ is not easy. The _tuition fees_ and the _cost of living_ are often very high. At first, many students _feel lonely_ and have _culture shock_. The _language barrier_ can also make daily life difficult.

Personally, I **have** never _studied_ abroad, but I **have** _read_ many stories from students who did. Most of them say the experience changed their lives in a good way.

***In conclusion***, although _studying abroad_ has some _disadvantages_, I believe the _advantages_ are more important. With good preparation, students can enjoy this amazing experience.

> **🔑 Từ nối:** On the one hand, In addition, On the other hand, In conclusion. **Present Perfect:** have never studied, have read. **Từ vựng:** advantages and disadvantages, high-quality education, degree, make international friends, career opportunities, graduate, tuition fees, cost of living, feel lonely, culture shock, language barrier.

## ✍️ BÀI 3 – Dạng KỂ TRẢI NGHIỆM (≈150 từ) — 🌟 nhiều Present Perfect
### *My experience preparing to study abroad*

I **have** always _dreamed_ of _studying abroad_, and this year my dream is coming true.

I **have** _prepared_ for a long time. First, I **have** _studied_ English for six years, and last month I **have passed** the IELTS exam. I **have** also _applied_ for a _scholarship_ at a university in Canada. Last week, I **have just received** a good news: I got the _admission letter_!

***However***, I feel a little nervous. I **have never lived** far from my family, so I may _feel homesick_. I **have never cooked** for myself either.

To get ready, I **have** started to learn cooking and to _manage my budget_. I **have** also joined an online group of Vietnamese students in Canada.

***In conclusion***, I **have** _worked_ hard for this chance. I believe _studying abroad_ will help me _grow_ and _open my mind_.

> **🔑 Present Perfect:** have always dreamed, have prepared, have studied, have applied, have never lived, have never cooked, have started, have joined, have worked. **Từ vựng:** study abroad, scholarship, admission letter, feel homesick, manage my budget, open my mind.
>
> ⚠️ *Lưu ý học thuật:* trong Bài 3, vài câu như *"last month I have passed"* và *"Last week, I have just received"* có **mốc thời gian quá khứ** (last month/last week) nên **văn chuẩn dùng Past Simple** (*passed / received*). Ở đây giữ Present Perfect để bạn **luyện nhận dạng** — hãy so sánh với **[`04-Ngu-phap.md`](04-Ngu-phap.md)** mục "Present Perfect vs Past Simple" và tự sửa lại cho đúng. 👉 Đây là **bài tập nhận biết lỗi** cố ý.

## 🧱 KHUNG VIẾT LUẬN (Essay template)

```
[Mở bài]   Nowadays, studying abroad is ________ (popular / a dream of many students).
           In my opinion, ________ (nêu quan điểm).

[Thân 1 – Lợi ích]
   Firstly, it helps students ________ . For example, ________ .
   Secondly, ________ .

[Thân 2 – Khó khăn / nhượng bộ]
   Of course / On the other hand, there are some challenges, such as ________ .
   However, ________ (the benefits are greater / with preparation, it's fine).

[Kết bài]  In conclusion, ________ . If I have the chance, I will ________ .
```

---

## 💡 Mẹo đạt B1 khi viết

1. **Mỗi đoạn có từ nối** ở đầu: *Firstly, Secondly, Moreover, However, In conclusion*.
2. Dùng **ít nhất 3 câu Present Perfect** (*have studied / have never been / have already applied*).
3. Chèn **1 câu nhượng bộ**: *Although… / On the other hand…* để nghe cân bằng.
4. Dùng **≥ 6 từ vựng** chủ đề (mục 1 của File 1).
5. Kết bài bằng **1 câu quan điểm cá nhân** rõ ràng.
6. Viết xong **đọc to** và kiểm tra: có nhầm Present Perfect với Past Simple không?

## ✅ Checklist trước khi nộp

- [ ] Có **3 phần** rõ ràng: mở – thân – kết.
- [ ] Có **≥ 3 câu Present Perfect** đúng (have/has + V3).
- [ ] Không nhầm: câu có *last year / in 2020 / ago* → phải dùng **Past Simple**.
- [ ] Có **≥ 1 câu nhượng bộ** (Although / However / On the other hand).
- [ ] Dùng **≥ 6 từ vựng** chủ đề du học.
- [ ] Độ dài **≈ 150–180 từ**.
