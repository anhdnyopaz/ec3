# 🎓 Topic – Study Abroad | File 7: BÀI LUẬN MẪU (bản dài ~4–5 phút)

> **Mục đích:** 3 bài luận mẫu **dài** (A2–B1) về du học — mỗi **nhận định tích cực (✅)** và **tiêu cực (❌)** đều có **ví dụ cụ thể** — kèm **khung viết** & **mẹo đạt B1**.
> **Quy ước:** **Present Perfect** in **đậm** · _từ vựng chủ đề_ in _nghiêng_ · ***từ nối/tương phản*** in đậm-nghiêng · 🟢 = ví dụ cho ý tích cực · 🔴 = ví dụ cho ý tiêu cực.
> **Độ dài:** mỗi bài ≈ **550–650 từ** → khi đọc/nói to (~130 từ/phút) sẽ **dài hơn 4 phút**.
> 💡 **Cách dùng ví dụ:** sau mỗi nhận định, luôn có 1 câu *"For example / For instance, …"* mô tả một tình huống **cụ thể, có thật** để người đọc hình dung được.

## ✍️ BÀI 1 – Dạng NÊU Ý KIẾN (≈600 từ, ~4.5 phút)
### *Should young people study abroad? — My opinion*

Every year, thousands of Vietnamese students leave home to _study abroad_. Some people think it is the best decision of their life, while others think it is a waste of money. In my opinion, _studying abroad_ is a wonderful chance for young people, ***although*** it also brings real challenges. In this essay, I will explain both the good and the bad sides, and I will give a clear example for each one.

***Firstly***, _studying abroad_ helps students _improve their language skills_ very quickly. **This is because** they use the language every day in real life, not only in a classroom.
🟢 **For example**, my cousin Lan went to Sydney two years ago. At first she could only say simple sentences, but she **has practised** English every day — ordering food, asking for directions, and doing group projects. Now she speaks so fluently that she **has even started** to dream in English.

***Secondly***, living in another country helps young people _gain independence_ and _broaden their horizons_. **This is because** they must take care of themselves without their parents.
🟢 **For instance**, a student who _studies abroad_ has to cook meals, wash clothes, pay bills, and wake up on time alone. My friend Minh **has told** me that after one year in Canada, he **has learned** to manage his own money and cook ten different dishes. He **has become** a completely independent person.

***Thirdly***, _studying abroad_ can give students a _high-quality education_ and _better career opportunities_. **This is because** many foreign universities have modern labs and famous teachers.
🟢 **For example**, students at top schools like MIT can use advanced technology that we do not have at home. Many companies, such as Google and Samsung, like to hire people who **have studied** overseas, because they have both knowledge and international experience.

***However***, I must be honest: _studying abroad_ is not always easy, and it has some serious _disadvantages_.

***The first problem*** is _financial pressure_. **This is because** the _tuition fees_ and _cost of living_ abroad are very high.
🔴 **For example**, one year at an American university can cost more than 30,000 dollars, plus rent, food, and flight tickets. Some families **have had to** sell their land or take a big loan to send just one child overseas.

***The second problem*** is _homesickness_ and _loneliness_. **This is because** students live far from their family and old friends.
🔴 **For instance**, during the first Tet holiday abroad, a student may sit alone in a cold room while their family is celebrating happily at home. Many students **have felt** so sad that they wanted to give up and fly back.

***Even so***, I still believe the benefits are greater than the difficulties. Most problems do not last long. ***Although*** students _feel homesick_ or have a _language barrier_ at the beginning, they usually _adapt_ within a few months and end up loving their new life.

***In conclusion***, _studying abroad_ helps young people _improve their language_, _gain independence_, and get a _high-quality education_, even though it can be expensive and lonely at first. In my view, if a student prepares carefully and _has_ a strong reason, the experience is worth every difficulty. I **have** always _dreamed_ of _studying abroad_, and after writing this essay, I feel even more sure that I want to try. Thank you for reading.

> **🔑 Present Perfect:** has practised, has even started, has told, has learned, has become, have studied, have had to, have felt, have dreamed. **Từ nối:** Firstly, Secondly, Thirdly, However, Even so, In conclusion. **Ví dụ:** 3 ví dụ 🟢 (tích cực) + 2 ví dụ 🔴 (tiêu cực), mỗi cái mở đầu bằng *For example / For instance*.

## ✍️ BÀI 2 – Dạng ƯU & NHƯỢC ĐIỂM (≈650 từ, ~5 phút) — 🌟 mỗi ý đều có ví dụ
### *The advantages and disadvantages of studying abroad*

_Studying abroad_ **has become** more and more popular among young Vietnamese people. Is it really a good idea? Like most big decisions, it has both clear _advantages_ and real _disadvantages_. In this essay, I will look at each side carefully and give a concrete example for every point.

### 🟢 The advantages (mặt tích cực)

***First of all***, _studying abroad_ helps students _enhance their language skills_. **This is because** they are surrounded by the language 24 hours a day.
🟢 **For example**, a student in London hears English on the bus, in shops, and at university. After six months, most students **have improved** their listening and speaking far more than they ever did at home.

***Secondly***, it helps young people _experience a new culture_ and _broaden their knowledge_. **This is because** they live among people who think and behave differently.
🟢 **For instance**, when my sister studied in Japan, she **has learned** to bow when greeting people, to be always on time, and to keep public places very clean. These small habits **have changed** the way she sees the world.

***Thirdly***, students can _make international friends_ and build a global network. **This is because** classrooms abroad are full of people from many countries.
🟢 **For example**, in one single class a student may sit next to friends from Korea, Brazil, and France. My teacher **has said** that some of her classmates from twenty years ago are now business partners in different countries.

***Finally***, a foreign _degree_ can _advance your future career_. **This is because** employers value international experience and _self-confidence_.
🟢 **For instance**, many big companies **have hired** graduates from foreign universities for higher salaries, because these students can work with people from all over the world.

### 🔴 The disadvantages (mặt tiêu cực)

***On the other hand***, _studying abroad_ has several _disadvantages_ that we cannot ignore.

***The first one*** is _financial pressure_. **This is because** the _tuition fees_ and living costs are extremely high.
🔴 **For example**, in Australia, a student may need about 20,000 dollars every year just for tuition, and even more for rent and food. Some parents **have spent** their whole savings to support one child.

***The second one*** is the _language barrier_ and _culture shock_ at the beginning. **This is because** everything — the accent, the food, the weather — is new and strange.
🔴 **For instance**, in the first weeks, a student may not understand a professor who speaks quickly, or may feel cold and lost during a long grey winter. This _culture shock_ can make daily life very stressful.

***The third one*** is _homesickness_ and the _academic challenge_. **This is because** students live far from family and must study very hard.
🔴 **For example**, foreign universities often ask for long essays and presentations every week. A student who is used to memorising may struggle, feel _lonely_, and _feel homesick_ at the same time.

### ⚖️ My conclusion

***To sum up***, _studying abroad_ has powerful _advantages_ — better language, a new culture, international friends, and career growth — but also real _disadvantages_ such as high cost, _culture shock_, and _homesickness_. ***In my opinion***, the _advantages_ are stronger, ***but only if*** the student prepares well. I **have** already _started_ to save money and _learn_ about my dream country, so I feel ready to enjoy the good sides and survive the hard ones. Thank you for reading.

> **🔑 Present Perfect:** has become, have improved, has learned, have changed, has said, have hired, have spent, have started. **Cấu trúc:** 4 ý ✅ (mỗi ý 1 ví dụ 🟢) + 3 ý ❌ (mỗi ý 1 ví dụ 🔴) + kết luận cân bằng. **Từ nối:** First of all, Secondly, Thirdly, Finally, On the other hand, To sum up.

## ✍️ BÀI 3 – Dạng KỂ TRẢI NGHIỆM (≈560 từ, ~4.5 phút)
### *My journey of preparing to study abroad*

I **have** always _dreamed_ of _studying abroad_, and this year my dream is slowly coming true. It **has not been** an easy journey, but every step **has taught** me something. In this essay, I want to share both the exciting and the difficult parts of my experience, with a real example for each.

At the beginning, everything felt positive. 🟢 **For example**, I **have studied** English for six years, and last year I finally passed the IELTS exam with a good score. That success **has given** me a lot of _self-confidence_. I **have also applied** for a _scholarship_ at a university in Canada, and two weeks ago I received an _admission letter_. When I read it, I was so happy that I cried.

Preparing has also helped me _grow_. 🟢 **For instance**, I **have learned** to cook simple meals, to _manage my budget_, and to wash my own clothes. I **have never** done these things before, because my mother always did them for me. Now I feel much more _independent_, and I know these skills will help me _adapt_ quickly.

***However***, the journey **has not been** only happy. There **have been** many worries too.

🔴 **For example**, the biggest problem **has been** money. The _tuition fees_ are very _expensive_, and my family **has had to** save carefully for years. Sometimes I feel guilty because my parents work so hard for me.

🔴 **For instance**, I also worry about _homesickness_. I **have never lived** far from my family, so I know I may _feel lonely_ during the first months. I **have already imagined** sitting alone in a small room, missing my mother's cooking and my friends' voices.

To prepare my mind for these hard moments, I **have** done a few things. 🟢 **For example**, I **have joined** an online group of Vietnamese students in Canada, and they **have shared** useful advice about the cold weather, the _language barrier_, and how to make friends. Their stories **have made** me feel less afraid.

***Of course***, I still feel nervous. ***However***, I believe I **have** done my best to get ready. _Studying abroad_ will help me _open my mind_, _experience a new culture_, and _broaden my horizons_. I **have** worked too hard to give up now.

***In conclusion***, my journey to _study abroad_ **has taught** me that big dreams need both hard work and courage. There **have been** happy moments, like passing IELTS and getting my _scholarship_, and hard moments, like the cost and the fear of _homesickness_. ***Although*** I know challenges are waiting, I am ready to face them. I **have** never felt so excited and so scared at the same time. Thank you for reading my story.

> **🔑 Present Perfect (rất nhiều):** have dreamed, has not been, has taught, have studied, has given, have applied, have learned, have never done, have been, has had to, have never lived, have already imagined, have joined, have shared, have made. **Ví dụ:** 🟢 (tích cực: IELTS, kỹ năng sống, nhóm hỗ trợ) + 🔴 (tiêu cực: tiền, nhớ nhà). **Từ nối:** However, Of course, In conclusion.
>
> ✅ *Lưu ý chuẩn:* toàn bộ câu Present Perfect trong 3 bài đều **không** đi kèm mốc thời gian quá khứ cụ thể. Chỉ những câu có *last year / two weeks ago* mới dùng **Past Simple** (*passed / received*) — hãy để ý cách dùng đúng này khi so với **[`04-Ngu-phap.md`](04-Ngu-phap.md)**.

## 🧱 KHUNG VIẾT LUẬN DÀI (Essay template ~4 phút)

```
[Mở bài ~5 câu]
   - Câu dẫn: Every year, many students ________ .
   - Nêu quan điểm: In my opinion, ________ .
   - Báo trước: I will explain both sides and give an example for each.

[Thân 1 – MẶT TÍCH CỰC ✅ (2–3 ý, MỖI Ý có ví dụ)]
   Firstly, ________ (nhận định tích cực). This is because ________ .
   🟢 For example, ________ (một tình huống cụ thể, có tên người/nơi/con số).
   Secondly, ________ . This is because ________ .
   🟢 For instance, ________ .

[Thân 2 – MẶT TIÊU CỰC ❌ (2–3 ý, MỖI Ý có ví dụ)]
   However, there are some problems.
   The first problem is ________ . This is because ________ .
   🔴 For example, ________ (con số / tình huống cụ thể).
   The second problem is ________ .
   🔴 For instance, ________ .

[Kết bài ~4 câu]
   To sum up, ________ (nhắc lại 2 mặt).
   In my opinion, ________ (quan điểm cuối + điều kiện: only if / as long as…).
```

---

## 💡 Mẹo đạt B1 khi viết bài dài + ví dụ

1. **Mỗi nhận định = 1 câu ý + 1 câu lý do (This is because…) + 1 câu ví dụ (For example…).** Đây là "công thức 3 câu" giúp bài dài ra tự nhiên.
2. **Ví dụ phải cụ thể:** có **tên người** (my cousin Lan), **nơi chốn** (Sydney, MIT), hoặc **con số** (30,000 dollars). Ví dụ chung chung sẽ mất điểm.
3. Cân bằng **✅ tích cực** và **❌ tiêu cực** — ít nhất 2 ý mỗi bên, mỗi ý một ví dụ.
4. Dùng **≥ 5 câu Present Perfect** (*have studied / has become / have never lived*).
5. Dùng **từ nối rõ ràng** ở đầu mỗi đoạn (Firstly, Secondly, However, To sum up).
6. Kết bài nêu **quan điểm có điều kiện**: *…the benefits are greater, **as long as** you prepare well.*
7. Viết xong **đọc to & bấm giờ** — mục tiêu **hơn 4 phút** là đạt độ dài.

## ✅ Checklist trước khi nộp (bài dài)

- [ ] Độ dài **≈ 550–650 từ** (đọc to **> 4 phút**).
- [ ] Có **≥ 2 nhận định tích cực ✅**, mỗi cái **1 ví dụ cụ thể** 🟢.
- [ ] Có **≥ 2 nhận định tiêu cực ❌**, mỗi cái **1 ví dụ cụ thể** 🔴.
- [ ] Mỗi ví dụ có **tên/nơi/con số** rõ ràng, mở đầu bằng *For example / For instance*.
- [ ] Có **≥ 5 câu Present Perfect** đúng (have/has + V3), không nhầm với Past Simple.
- [ ] Có **≥ 1 câu nhượng bộ** (Although / However / On the other hand).
- [ ] Kết bài có **quan điểm cá nhân** rõ ràng.
