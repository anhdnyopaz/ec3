# 🎓 Topic – Study Abroad | File 5: BÀI TẬP (có đáp án)

> **Cách dùng:** làm hết bài **trước**, rồi mới xem **ĐÁP ÁN** ở cuối file. Làm bằng bút, đọc to đáp án của bạn.
> Trọng tâm: **từ vựng du học** + **Present Perfect** (have/has + V3).

### Bài 2. Điền từ vào chỗ trống (dùng từ cho sẵn)

> *culture shock · scholarship · independent · homesick · abroad*

1. I want to study ________ to see the world.
2. She won a ________ , so she doesn't pay tuition fees.
3. In the first week, he felt ________ and missed his mom.
4. New food and customs gave me ________ at first.
5. Living alone made me more ________ .

---

## 📝 PHẦN B – PRESENT PERFECT

### Bài 3. Chia động từ ở dạng đúng (Present Perfect: have/has + V3)

1. I ________ (apply) for three universities so far.
2. She ________ (never / be) to Europe.
3. ________ you ________ (get) your visa yet?
4. We ________ (live) here since 2022.
5. He ________ (just / receive) his admission letter.
6. They ________ (not / decide) on a major yet.

### Bài 4. Chọn *for* hoặc *since*

1. I have studied English ________ ten years.
2. She has been abroad ________ last September.
3. We have known each other ________ high school.
4. He has lived in Canada ________ six months.

### Bài 5. Chọn *Present Perfect* hay *Past Simple*

1. I (have visited / visited) Paris **last year**.
2. She (has never eaten / never ate) sushi in her life.
3. They (have moved / moved) to London **in 2021**.
4. (Have you ever been / Did you ever be) to Australia?

---

## 📝 PHẦN C – SỬA LỖI

### Bài 6. Tìm và sửa 1 lỗi trong mỗi câu

1. I have **went** to Japan twice.
2. She **have** already applied for a scholarship.
3. I have lived here **since** three years.
4. Have you finished your application **already**? *(đây là câu hỏi trung tính)*
5. He have been abroad **last month**.

---

## 📝 PHẦN D – VIẾT CÂU

### Bài 7. Viết câu Present Perfect theo gợi ý

1. (I / never / study / abroad) → ________
2. (you / ever / win / a scholarship?) → ________
3. (She / just / get / her visa) → ________
4. (We / live / here / for / two years) → ________

---
---

# ✅ ĐÁP ÁN

## Phần A
**Bài 1:** 1-b · 2-d · 3-a · 4-e · 5-c
**Bài 2:** 1. abroad · 2. scholarship · 3. homesick · 4. culture shock · 5. independent

## Phần B
**Bài 3:**
1. have applied
2. has never been
3. Have … got
4. have lived
5. has just received
6. haven't decided (have not decided)

**Bài 4:** 1. for · 2. since · 3. since · 4. for

**Bài 5:**
1. **visited** (có "last year")
2. **has never eaten** (kinh nghiệm, không mốc thời gian)
3. **moved** (có "in 2021")
4. **Have you ever been** (câu hỏi kinh nghiệm)

## Phần C
**Bài 6:**
1. have **gone / been** (không phải *went*)
2. She **has** already applied
3. …**for** three years (for + độ dài)
4. Have you finished your application **yet**?
5. He **was** abroad last month. *(có "last month" → Past Simple)*

## Phần D
**Bài 7:** (đáp án tham khảo)
1. I have never studied abroad.
2. Have you ever won a scholarship?
3. She has just got her visa.
4. We have lived here for two years.
