# 🎓 Topic – Study Abroad | File 3: HỘI THOẠI MẪU

> **Mục đích:** xem cách lắp **từ vựng (File 1)** + **mẫu câu (File 2)** + **Present Perfect (File 4)** vào hội thoại thật.
> **Quy ước in chữ:** _từ vựng chủ đề_ in _nghiêng_ · **Present Perfect** (have/has + V3) in **đậm**.
> **Cách dùng:** (1) Đọc hiểu → (2) Đóng vai / nói lại → (3) Đổi thông tin thành chuyện của bạn.

---

## 💬 Hội thoại 1 — *"Have you ever thought about studying abroad?"*

> **Hoa:** Hi Nam! I heard you want to _study abroad_. Is that true?
> **Nam:** Yes! I **have always dreamed** of studying in Australia.
> **Hoa:** That's great. **Have you** ever **been** overseas before?
> **Nam:** No, I **haven't**. I **have never lived** far from my family, so I'm a little nervous.
> **Hoa:** Don't worry. Many students _feel homesick_ at first, but they soon _adapt_.
> **Nam:** I hope so. I want to _improve my language skills_ and _experience a new culture_.
> **Hoa:** Good reasons! **Have you applied** for a _scholarship_ yet?
> **Nam:** Yes, I **have already applied**. I'm waiting for the result now.
> **Hoa:** Fingers crossed! Studying abroad will _broaden your horizons_.

> **🔑 Present Perfect:** have always dreamed, Have you been, haven't, have never lived, Have you applied, have already applied. **Từ vựng:** study abroad, feel homesick, adapt, improve language skills, experience a new culture, scholarship, broaden horizons.

---

## 💬 Hội thoại 2 — *"What are the pros and cons?"* (Cân nhắc ưu/nhược)

> **Linh:** My parents want me to _study abroad_, but I'm not sure. What do you think?
> **Mai:** Well, it has both _advantages and disadvantages_. On the one hand, you get a _high-quality education_.
> **Linh:** True. And I would _make international friends_.
> **Mai:** Exactly. On the other hand, the _tuition fees_ are _expensive_, and you may _feel lonely_ at first.
> **Linh:** That's my worry. I **have never been** away from home for so long.
> **Mai:** I understand. But **have you thought** about the _career opportunities_ after you _graduate_?
> **Linh:** Yes, I **have**. A degree from abroad can help me get a better job.
> **Mai:** Right. Although it is hard at first, I think it is _worth it_.

> **🔑 Present Perfect:** have never been, have you thought, have. **Tương phản:** On the one hand… On the other hand…, Although. **Từ vựng:** advantages and disadvantages, high-quality education, make international friends, tuition fees, feel lonely, career opportunities, graduate.

---

## 💬 Hội thoại 3 — *"How is your life abroad?"* (Kể trải nghiệm khi đã đi)

> **Tom:** Hi Linh! You **have been** in London for three months now. How is it?
> **Linh:** It's amazing! I **have learned** so much. But the first weeks were hard.
> **Tom:** Really? What happened?
> **Linh:** I had _culture shock_, and there was a _language barrier_. I couldn't understand people's accents.
> **Tom:** And now?
> **Linh:** Much better! I **have made** many _international friends_, and I **have joined** a cooking club.
> **Tom:** Wow, you **have adapted** quickly!
> **Linh:** Yes. Living here **has made** me more _independent_. I cook, clean, and manage my own money now.
> **Tom:** That's wonderful. You **have really grown**.

> **🔑 Present Perfect:** have been, have learned, have made, have joined, have adapted, has made, have grown. **Từ vựng:** culture shock, language barrier, international friends, independent.

---

## 💬 Hội thoại 4 — *"Any advice before I go?"* (Xin lời khuyên trước khi đi)

> **Bình:** I will _study abroad_ next month. **Have you** got any advice?
> **Anh:** Sure! First, **have you learned** about the local _culture_ yet?
> **Bình:** A little. I **have watched** some videos about daily life there.
> **Anh:** Good. You should also _make friends_ early, so you won't _feel lonely_.
> **Bình:** Okay. What about money?
> **Anh:** The _cost of living_ is high, so _manage your budget_ carefully. **Have you opened** a bank account?
> **Bình:** Not yet. I **haven't done** that.
> **Anh:** Do it soon. And remember: everyone _feels homesick_ sometimes — it's normal.
> **Bình:** Thank you! I feel more confident now.

> **🔑 Present Perfect:** Have you got, have you learned, have watched, Have you opened, haven't done. **Từ vựng:** study abroad, culture, make friends, feel lonely, cost of living, manage your budget, feel homesick.

## 🎭 Bài tập ĐÓNG VAI (Role-play)

1. **Đóng vai A & B** trong Hội thoại 1, sau đó **đổi vai**. Thu âm giọng mình.
2. **Thay thông tin thật:** đổi *Australia* → nước bạn muốn đi; đổi *scholarship* → việc bạn đã chuẩn bị.
3. **Tự tạo hội thoại mới** theo khung:
```
A: Have you ever thought about studying abroad?
B: Yes, I have always dreamed of studying in ________ .
A: Have you ________ (applied / prepared / studied…) yet?
B: Yes, I have already ________ . / No, I haven't ________ yet.
A: What do you want to study, and why?
B: I want to study ________ because ________ .
```

## ✅ Checklist sau khi luyện hội thoại

- [ ] Đọc trôi chảy cả 4 hội thoại, hiểu nghĩa.
- [ ] Nói được **≥ 3 câu Present Perfect** (have/has + V3).
- [ ] Dùng đúng câu hỏi **Have you ever…?** và trả lời ngắn **Yes, I have / No, I haven't**.
- [ ] Đóng vai được **1 hội thoại** không nhìn giấy.
