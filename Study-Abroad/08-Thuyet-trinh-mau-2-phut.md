# 🎓 Topic – Study Abroad | File 8: THUYẾT TRÌNH MẪU ~2 PHÚT

> **Mục đích:** 3 bài thuyết trình ~2 phút (≈ 240–270 từ) về du học + khung điền + mẹo trình bày.
> **Quy ước:** **Present Perfect** in **đậm** · _từ vựng chủ đề_ in _nghiêng_ · ***từ nối/tương phản*** in đậm-nghiêng.
> Mỗi bài chia **Mở → 3 ý → Kết** để dễ nhớ.

---

## 🎤 Bài 1 — *Why students should study abroad* (≈255 từ)

> Hello everyone! My name is ______. Today, I'd like to talk about _studying abroad_ and why it is a great choice for young people.

> These days, more and more students _study abroad_. I **have** always _dreamed_ of it, and I believe it brings many benefits. So, why should we _study abroad_?

> ***Firstly***, it helps us _improve our language skills_. When you live in another country, you speak English every day. ***Because of this***, you learn much faster than in a classroom at home.

> ***Secondly***, _studying abroad_ lets you _experience a new culture_ and _broaden your horizons_. You try new food, celebrate new festivals, and _make international friends_. I **have** _met_ many foreign students online, and their stories are amazing.

> ***Thirdly***, living far from home teaches you to be _independent_. You cook, clean, and _manage your budget_ by yourself. ***In this way***, you _grow up_ and become _self-reliant_.

> ***Of course***, there are challenges. Students may _feel homesick_ or have _culture shock_ at first. ***However***, these problems do not last long. Most students soon _adapt_ and love their new life.

> ***In conclusion***, _studying abroad_ helps us _improve our language_, _experience a new culture_, and become _independent_. It is a _once-in-a-lifetime experience_. If you have the chance, take it! Thank you for listening!

> **🔑 Present Perfect:** have always dreamed, have met. **Từ nối:** Firstly, Secondly, Thirdly, Because of this, In this way, Of course, However, In conclusion. **Từ vựng:** study abroad, improve language skills, experience a new culture, broaden horizons, make international friends, independent, manage budget, feel homesick, culture shock, adapt.

## 🎤 Bài 2 — *Studying abroad: benefits and challenges* (≈250 từ)

> Hello everyone! Today I'd like to talk about the two sides of _studying abroad_: its benefits and its challenges.

> _Studying abroad_ **has become** very popular, but is it always easy? Let's look at both sides.

> ***On the one hand***, there are big benefits. Students get a _high-quality education_ and a valuable _degree_. They also gain _better career opportunities_ after they _graduate_. ***In addition***, they _make international friends_ and _open their minds_ to the world.

> ***On the other hand***, _studying abroad_ has some challenges. The _tuition fees_ and _cost of living_ are often _expensive_. At the beginning, many students _feel lonely_ and have a _language barrier_. They also miss their families.

> So, how can we deal with these challenges? ***First***, you should _apply for a scholarship_ to save money. ***Second***, you should _make friends_ early, so you won't _feel isolated_. I **have** _read_ that students who join clubs _adapt_ much faster.

> ***In my opinion***, the benefits are greater than the difficulties. With good preparation, anyone can enjoy this experience. I **have** never _studied_ abroad yet, but I _have_ _prepared_ carefully, and I feel ready.

> ***In conclusion***, _studying abroad_ has both good and hard sides. ***However***, if we prepare well, it becomes a wonderful journey. Thank you for listening!

> **🔑 Present Perfect:** has become, have read, have never studied, have prepared. **Từ nối:** On the one hand, On the other hand, In addition, First, Second, In my opinion, In conclusion, However. **Từ vựng:** high-quality education, degree, career opportunities, graduate, tuition fees, cost of living, feel lonely, language barrier, scholarship, feel isolated, adapt.

## 🎤 Bài 3 — *My journey to study abroad* (≈245 từ) — 🌟 nhiều Present Perfect

> Hello everyone! Today I'd like to share my personal journey: how I **have** _prepared_ to _study abroad_.

> Ever since I was a child, I **have** _dreamed_ of studying in another country. But a dream is not enough — you must work hard. So, what **have** I _done_ to reach my goal?

> ***First***, I **have** _studied_ English very seriously. I **have** _learned_ English for more than six years, and I **have** _improved_ my speaking a lot. Language is the key to _studying abroad_.

> ***Second***, I **have** _saved_ money and _applied_ for a _scholarship_. ***Because*** the _tuition fees_ are _expensive_, a scholarship can really help. Last month I **have sent** my _application_, and now I am waiting for the result.

> ***Third***, I **have** _prepared_ my mind. I **have never lived** far from my family, so I know I may _feel homesick_. To get ready, I **have** _learned_ to cook and to _manage my budget_.

> ***Of course***, I still feel a little nervous. ***However***, I believe I **have** _done_ my best. _Studying abroad_ will help me _grow_, _open my mind_, and _experience a new culture_.

> ***In conclusion***, my journey **has** not been easy, but it **has** taught me to never give up. Thank you for listening!

> **🔑 Present Perfect:** have prepared, have dreamed, have done, have studied, have learned, have improved, have saved, have applied, have sent, have never lived, has been, has taught. **Từ vựng:** study abroad, scholarship, tuition fees, application, feel homesick, manage budget, open my mind, experience a new culture.
>
> ⚠️ *Lưu ý:* câu *"Last month I have sent"* có mốc **last month** → văn chuẩn dùng **Past Simple** (*sent*). Giữ ở đây để bạn tự nhận ra & sửa (xem File 4).

## 📊 BONUS – Cụm từ MÔ TẢ BIỂU ĐỒ & SỐ LIỆU (khi thuyết trình có slide/chart)

> Khi bài thuyết trình du học của bạn có **biểu đồ** (ví dụ: số sinh viên đi du học mỗi năm, chi phí, tỷ lệ...), dùng các cụm sau để dẫn dắt khán giả nhìn vào số liệu.

| English | Nghĩa |
|---------|-------|
| First, let me quickly explain the graph. | Trước tiên, để tôi giải thích nhanh biểu đồ. |
| The graph shows our figures. | Biểu đồ cho thấy các số liệu của chúng ta. |
| Let's now look at the next slide / point. | Bây giờ, hãy xem slide / phần tiếp theo. |
| I'd like you to focus your attention on… | Tôi muốn các bạn tập trung chú ý vào… |
| Let's look more closely at… | Hãy nhìn kỹ hơn vào… |
| What I'd like to point out here is… | Điều tôi muốn chỉ ra ở đây là… |
| I think you'll be surprised to see… | Tôi nghĩ các bạn sẽ ngạc nhiên khi thấy… |
| You can see that different colors have been used to indicate… | Các bạn có thể thấy các màu khác nhau được dùng để biểu thị… |

> 💬 **Ví dụ dùng khi có biểu đồ:** *First, let me quickly explain the graph. **The graph shows** the number of Vietnamese students who **have studied** abroad since 2015. **I'd like you to focus your attention on** the big increase after 2020.*

## 🧱 KHUNG THUYẾT TRÌNH của bạn (2 phút)

```
[Mở đầu]   Hello everyone! Today I'd like to talk about studying abroad.
[Dẫn]      Studying abroad has become popular, and I have always dreamed of it.
[Ý 1]      Firstly, it helps us ________ . If you ________ , you will ________ .
[Ý 2]      Secondly, ________ . I have ________ (Present Perfect) …
[Ý 3]      Thirdly, ________ .
[Nhượng bộ] Of course, there are challenges such as ________ . However, ________ .
[Kết]      In conclusion, studying abroad ________ . If you have the chance, take it!
           Thank you for listening!
```

---

## 💡 Mẹo trình bày (Delivery tips)

1. **Chào + giới thiệu chủ đề** trong 2 câu đầu, nói chậm & rõ.
2. Dùng **từ nối đánh số**: *Firstly, Secondly, Thirdly* — khán giả dễ theo dõi.
3. Có **≥ 3 câu Present Perfect** để kể kinh nghiệm (*I have studied…*).
4. Chèn **1 đoạn nhượng bộ** (*Of course… However…*) để nghe cân bằng.
5. **Giao tiếp bằng mắt**, mỉm cười, dùng tay khi nhấn ý.
6. Kết bằng câu **kêu gọi** (*If you have the chance, take it!*) rồi **cảm ơn**.

## ✅ Checklist trước khi thuyết trình

- [ ] Có **mở – 3 ý – kết** rõ ràng.
- [ ] Dùng **≥ 3 câu Present Perfect** (have/has + V3).
- [ ] Có **≥ 5 từ nối** (Firstly, Secondly, However, In conclusion…).
- [ ] Có **1 đoạn nhượng bộ** (Of course… However…).
- [ ] Dùng **≥ 6 từ vựng** chủ đề du học.
- [ ] Nói được **≈ 2 phút** không nhìn giấy.
