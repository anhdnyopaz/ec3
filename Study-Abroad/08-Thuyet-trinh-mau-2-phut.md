# 🎓 Topic – Study Abroad | File 8: THUYẾT TRÌNH MẪU ~4 PHÚT (bản dài, có ví dụ)

> **Mục đích:** 3 bài thuyết trình **dài ~4 phút** (≈ 500–560 từ) về du học — mỗi **nhận định tích cực (✅)** và **tiêu cực (❌)** đều có **ví dụ cụ thể** — + cụm mô tả biểu đồ + khung + mẹo trình bày.
> **Quy ước:** **Present Perfect** in **đậm** · _từ vựng chủ đề_ in _nghiêng_ · ***từ nối/tương phản*** in đậm-nghiêng · 🟢 = ví dụ tích cực · 🔴 = ví dụ tiêu cực.
> **Độ dài:** ~500–560 từ → nói to (~130 từ/phút) sẽ **dài hơn 4 phút**. Mỗi bài chia **Mở → các ý (kèm ví dụ) → Nhượng bộ → Kết**.

## 🎤 Bài 1 — *Why students should study abroad* (≈530 từ, ~4 phút)

> Hello everyone! My name is ______. Today, I'd like to talk about _studying abroad_ and why it is a great choice for young people. I **have** always _dreamed_ of it, and I will give you a real example for each of my points.

> ***Firstly***, _studying abroad_ helps us _improve our language skills_ very fast. This is because we use the language every single day, not only in a classroom.
> 🟢 **For example**, my cousin Lan went to Sydney two years ago. At first she could only say simple sentences. But she **has practised** English every day — buying food, asking for directions, and joining group projects — and now she speaks so fluently that she **has even started** to dream in English.

> ***Secondly***, _studying abroad_ lets us _experience a new culture_ and _broaden our horizons_.
> 🟢 **For instance**, when my sister studied in Japan, she **has learned** to bow when she meets people, to always be on time, and to keep public places clean. These small habits **have changed** the way she sees the world.

> ***Thirdly***, living far from home teaches us to be _independent_ and _self-reliant_.
> 🟢 **For example**, a student abroad must cook, clean, pay bills, and wake up alone. My friend Minh **has told** me that after one year in Canada, he **has learned** to _manage his budget_ and cook ten dishes. He **has become** a truly independent person.

> ***Of course***, I must be honest — _studying abroad_ also has some hard sides.

> ***One challenge*** is _financial pressure_, because the _tuition fees_ are very _expensive_.
> 🔴 **For instance**, one year at an American university can cost more than 30,000 dollars, plus rent and flights. Some families **have had to** take a big loan for just one child.

> ***Another challenge*** is _homesickness_.
> 🔴 **For example**, during the first Tet holiday abroad, a student may sit alone in a cold room while the family celebrates at home. Many students **have felt** very sad at that moment.

> ***However***, these problems do not last long. Most students soon _adapt_ and love their new life.

> ***In conclusion***, _studying abroad_ helps us _improve our language_, _experience a new culture_, and become _independent_ — even if it is expensive and lonely at first. It is a _once-in-a-lifetime experience_. If you have the chance, take it! Thank you for listening!

> **🔑 Present Perfect:** have dreamed, has practised, has even started, has learned, have changed, has told, has become, have had to, have felt. **Ví dụ:** 3 🟢 + 2 🔴. **Từ nối:** Firstly, Secondly, Thirdly, Of course, However, In conclusion.

## 🎤 Bài 2 — *Studying abroad: benefits and challenges* (≈540 từ, ~4 phút) — 🌟 mỗi ý một ví dụ

> Hello everyone! Today I'd like to talk about the two sides of _studying abroad_: its benefits and its challenges. _Studying abroad_ **has become** very popular, but is it always easy? Let's look at both sides, with a clear example for each.

> ***On the one hand***, there are big _benefits_.

> ***First***, students get a _high-quality education_.
> 🟢 **For example**, top universities like MIT have modern labs and famous professors. Companies such as Google and Samsung **have hired** many graduates from these schools for high salaries.

> ***Second***, students _make international friends_ and build a global network.
> 🟢 **For instance**, in one class you may sit next to friends from Korea, Brazil, and France. My teacher **has said** that some of her old classmates are now business partners in different countries.

> ***Third***, _studying abroad_ builds _self-confidence_.
> 🟢 **For example**, a shy student who **has lived** and studied overseas for a year usually comes home able to speak in public and solve problems alone.

> ***On the other hand***, _studying abroad_ has real _challenges_.

> ***First***, there is _financial pressure_.
> 🔴 **For example**, in Australia a student may need about 20,000 dollars a year just for tuition, and more for rent and food. Some parents **have spent** their whole savings.

> ***Second***, there is a _language barrier_ and _culture shock_ at the start.
> 🔴 **For instance**, in the first weeks a student may not understand a professor who speaks quickly, or may feel cold and lost during a long grey winter.

> ***Third***, there is _homesickness_ and the _academic challenge_.
> 🔴 **For example**, foreign universities often ask for long essays every week, so a student may feel _lonely_ and stressed at the same time.

> So, how can we deal with these challenges? You should _apply for a scholarship_ to save money, and _make friends_ early. I **have** _read_ that students who join clubs _adapt_ much faster.

> ***In conclusion***, _studying abroad_ has strong benefits and real challenges. ***In my opinion***, the benefits are greater — ***as long as*** we prepare well. Thank you for listening!

> **🔑 Present Perfect:** has become, have hired, has said, has lived, have spent, have read. **Cấu trúc:** 3 ý ✅ (mỗi ý 🟢) + 3 ý ❌ (mỗi ý 🔴). **Từ nối:** On the one hand, On the other hand, First/Second/Third, In conclusion.

## 🎤 Bài 3 — *My journey to study abroad* (≈510 từ, ~4 phút) — 🌟 nhiều Present Perfect

> Hello everyone! Today I'd like to share my personal journey: how I **have** _prepared_ to _study abroad_, and the happy and hard moments along the way.

> Ever since I was a child, I **have** _dreamed_ of studying in another country. But a dream is not enough — you must work hard. So, what **have** I _done_ to reach my goal?

> The good news first. 🟢 **For example**, I **have studied** English for more than six years, and I **have improved** my speaking a lot. Last year I finally passed the IELTS exam with a good score, and that success **has given** me real _self-confidence_.

> 🟢 **For instance**, I **have also learned** important life skills. I **have never cooked** or _managed my own budget_ before, because my mother always helped me. Now I can cook simple meals and save money — so I feel more _independent_ already.

> 🟢 **For example**, I **have just applied** for a _scholarship_ at a university in Canada, and last week I received an _admission letter_. When I read it, I was so happy that I cried.

> ***However***, my journey **has not been** only happy. There **have been** worries too.

> 🔴 **For instance**, the biggest problem **has been** money. The _tuition fees_ are _expensive_, and my family **has had to** save carefully for years. Sometimes I feel guilty because my parents work so hard for me.

> 🔴 **For example**, I also worry about _homesickness_. I **have never lived** far from my family, so I know I may _feel lonely_ in the first months, missing my mother's cooking and my friends' voices.

> To get ready for these hard moments, I **have** _joined_ an online group of Vietnamese students in Canada. They **have shared** useful advice about the cold weather and the _language barrier_, and their stories **have made** me feel less afraid.

> ***In conclusion***, my journey **has** taught me that big dreams need both hard work and courage. There **have been** happy moments, like passing IELTS, and hard moments, like the cost and the fear of _homesickness_. ***Although*** challenges are waiting, I am ready to face them. Thank you for listening!

> **🔑 Present Perfect:** have prepared, have dreamed, have done, have studied, have improved, has given, have learned, have never cooked, have just applied, has not been, have been, has had to, have never lived, have joined, have shared, have made, has taught. **Ví dụ:** 🟢 (IELTS, kỹ năng sống, học bổng) + 🔴 (tiền, nhớ nhà). **Từ nối:** However, In conclusion, Although.

## 📊 BONUS – Cụm từ MÔ TẢ BIỂU ĐỒ & SỐ LIỆU (khi thuyết trình có slide/chart)

> Khi bài thuyết trình du học của bạn có **biểu đồ** (ví dụ: số sinh viên đi du học mỗi năm, chi phí, tỷ lệ...), dùng các cụm sau để dẫn dắt khán giả nhìn vào số liệu.

| English | Nghĩa |
|---------|-------|

## 🧱 KHUNG THUYẾT TRÌNH của bạn (~4 phút, có ví dụ)

```
[Mở đầu]   Hello everyone! Today I'd like to talk about studying abroad.
           I have always dreamed of it, and I will give an example for each point.

[Ý tích cực 1] Firstly, it helps us ________ . This is because ________ .
   🟢 For example, ________ (tên người/nơi/con số cụ thể).
[Ý tích cực 2] Secondly, ________ .
   🟢 For instance, ________ .

[Ý tiêu cực 1] Of course, one challenge is ________ .
   🔴 For example, ________ (con số / tình huống cụ thể).
[Ý tiêu cực 2] Another challenge is ________ .
   🔴 For instance, ________ .

[Nhượng bộ] However, these problems do not last long. Most students soon adapt.
[Kết]      In conclusion, studying abroad ________ . If you have the chance, take it!
           Thank you for listening!
```

## 💡 Mẹo trình bày (Delivery tips)

1. **Chào + giới thiệu chủ đề** trong 2 câu đầu, nói chậm & rõ.
2. Dùng **từ nối đánh số**: *Firstly, Secondly, Thirdly* — khán giả dễ theo dõi.
3. **Sau mỗi nhận định, dừng một nhịp rồi nói "For example…"** — ví dụ cụ thể là phần khán giả nhớ nhất.
4. Có **≥ 4 câu Present Perfect** để kể kinh nghiệm (*I have studied…*).
5. Chèn **1 đoạn nhượng bộ** (*Of course… However…*) để nghe cân bằng.
6. **Giao tiếp bằng mắt**, mỉm cười, dùng tay khi nhấn ý.
7. Kết bằng câu **kêu gọi** (*If you have the chance, take it!*) rồi **cảm ơn**.

## ✅ Checklist trước khi thuyết trình (bản ~4 phút)

- [ ] Độ dài **≈ 500–560 từ** (nói to **> 4 phút**).
- [ ] Có **≥ 2 nhận định tích cực ✅**, mỗi cái **1 ví dụ cụ thể 🟢**.
- [ ] Có **≥ 2 nhận định tiêu cực ❌**, mỗi cái **1 ví dụ cụ thể 🔴**.
- [ ] Mỗi ví dụ có **tên/nơi/con số**, mở đầu bằng *For example / For instance*.
- [ ] Dùng **≥ 4 câu Present Perfect** (have/has + V3), không nhầm Past Simple.
- [ ] Có **1 đoạn nhượng bộ** (Of course… However…).
- [ ] Có **mở – các ý – kết** rõ ràng; kết bằng câu kêu gọi.
