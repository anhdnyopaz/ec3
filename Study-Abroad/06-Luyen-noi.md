# 🎓 Topic – Study Abroad | File 6: LUYỆN NÓI

> **Mục tiêu:** nói được **1–2 phút** về du học, dùng **từ vựng chủ đề** + **Present Perfect**, mà **không nhìn giấy**.
> Cách luyện: đọc câu hỏi → **nói thành tiếng** → thu âm → nghe lại → nói lại tốt hơn.

---

## 🗣️ PHẦN A – CÂU HỎI LUYỆN NÓI (Speaking questions)

### Nhóm 1 – Dự định & lý do
1. Have you ever thought about studying abroad?
2. Which country would you like to study in? Why?
3. What do you want to study, and why?
4. Have you done anything to prepare? (learn English, save money…)

### Nhóm 2 – Lợi ích & khó khăn
5. What are the benefits of studying abroad?
6. What problems might students face abroad?
7. Would you feel homesick? How would you deal with it?
8. Is studying abroad worth the money? Why / why not?

### Nhóm 3 – Kinh nghiệm (Present Perfect)
9. Have you ever traveled to another country? Where?
10. Have you ever lived far from your family?
11. How long have you studied English?
12. Have you already chosen a dream university?

> 💡 **Mẹo trả lời "ăn điểm":** với nhóm 3, luôn mở đầu bằng **Present Perfect**:
> *"Yes, I have… / No, I have never…"* rồi **thêm 1–2 câu chi tiết**.

---

## 🎯 PHẦN B – KHUNG TRẢ LỜI mẫu (nói theo là được)

### Khung 1 – Trả lời câu "Why do you want to study abroad?"
```
Well, I have always dreamed of studying abroad in ________ .
There are a few reasons. First, I want to ________ (improve my language skills).
Second, it will help me ________ (gain independence / broaden my horizons).
I have already ________ (studied English for … years / applied for a scholarship),
so I feel ready. Of course, I may ________ (feel homesick) at first,
but I think it is worth it.
```

### Khung 2 – Trả lời câu "Have you ever lived far from home?"
```
No, I haven't. / Yes, I have.
I have never lived far from my family before, so I feel a little nervous.
But I have prepared by ________ (learning to cook / managing my money).
I believe I will adapt quickly.
```

---

## 🎤 PHẦN C – BÀI NÓI MẪU ~1 PHÚT (≈130 từ)

## *Why I want to study abroad*

> Hello! Today I'd like to talk about my dream: studying abroad.

> I **have always dreamed** of studying in Australia. There are three main reasons. First, I want to **improve my language skills** and speak English fluently. Second, studying abroad will help me **experience a new culture** and **broaden my horizons**. Third, a degree from abroad can give me **better career opportunities**.

> I **have already prepared** a lot. I **have studied** English for six years, and I **have just applied** for a scholarship. Of course, I know I may **feel homesick** at first, because I **have never lived** far from my family. But I believe I will soon **adapt** and **become more independent**.

> Studying abroad is a once-in-a-lifetime experience, and I can't wait. Thank you!

> **🔑 Present Perfect:** have always dreamed, have already prepared, have studied, have just applied, have never lived. **Từ vựng:** improve language skills, experience a new culture, broaden horizons, career opportunities, feel homesick, adapt, independent.

---

## 🧱 PHẦN D – TỰ KỂ (điền khung của bạn)

```
Hi, my name is ________ .
I have always wanted to study ________ (major) in ________ (country).
The main reason is ________ .
To prepare, I have ________ (present perfect: studied / saved / applied…).
I have never ________ before, so I feel ________ .
But studying abroad will help me ________ , so I am excited.
Thank you for listening!
```

---

## 📅 Lộ trình luyện 3 ngày

| Ngày | Việc cần làm |
|------|--------------|
| 1 | Trả lời **Nhóm 1 & 2** (câu 1–8) thành tiếng, mỗi câu 2–3 câu trả lời |
| 2 | Trả lời **Nhóm 3** (câu 9–12) — bắt buộc dùng Present Perfect |
| 3 | Học thuộc **bài nói mẫu 1 phút** → nói lại bằng lời của bạn, không nhìn giấy |

---

## ✅ Checklist trước khi nói

- [ ] Dùng **≥ 5 từ vựng** chủ đề du học.
- [ ] Dùng **≥ 3 câu Present Perfect** (have/has + V3).
- [ ] Có **1 câu "have never…"** (điều chưa từng làm).
- [ ] Có **1 câu lý do** (because / to / so that).
- [ ] Nói được **≈ 1 phút** không nhìn giấy.
